October News
Faction Wars
Faction Wars were an exciting attempt at a series od Discord mingames, puzzles and challenges. It brought community members together in the form of 9 different factions - with a twist! Humans had the ability to pledge their alleigance to any faction, and owners of multiple tokens had the choice of multiple factions. It was clear from the getgo that us Zombies were outnumbered, and while the official count of each faction was never officially posted, Beasts, Humans and Jellies certainly came in stronger. Each day was made up of riddle, paired with 25 quests that could be completed over the course of the week. These challenges kept us busy and ultimately led to a really fun environment in Discord that week. Here are the final totals from faction wars, aliong with some highlights: Ultimately the Beasts came out on top , followed by Humans and Mechs. Each faction also elected a Leader and and MVP based on performance. Near the end of faction Wars, it was tocuhed on that three separate new tokens would be awarded to different "winners" from the war. The results of the tokens are yet to be known.
