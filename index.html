October News
Faction Wars

Faction Wars were an exciting attempt at a series of Discord minigames, puzzles and challenges. It brought community members together in the form of 9 different factions - with a twist! Humans had the ability to pledge their allegiance to any faction, and owners of multiple tokens had the choice of multiple factions. This was the first real token mechanic we have seen from the team on their website, and it had a unique experience for each faction. The main component of the war was the Sekai Hunt, and brought these new-found groups together to earn points for an unknown prize. Each day was made up of a challenge that unlocked a riddle upon completion. The first team to complete the riddle earned 100 points, followed by the second team at 90 points, and so on. Each quest was also worth 20 points and could be completed at any time.

These challenges kept us busy and ultimately led to a really fun environment in Discord. Here are the final point toals from the Sekai Hunt:

  1. Beasts: 963 points
  2. Demons: 844 points
  3. Puppets: 794 points
  4. Humans: 755 points
  5. Jellies: 714 points
  6. Mechs: 628 points
  7. Angels: 474 points
  8. Zombies: 452 points
  9. Dragons: 275 points

Ultimately the Beasts came out on top , followed by Demons and Puppets. Each faction also elected a Leader and a MVP based on performance. Near the end of Faction War, it was tocuhed on by the team that three separate new tokens would be awarded to different "winners" from the war. The contents/utility of the tokens are yet to be known.

This event was paired with its own set of lore and art which accompanied our challenges. We have yet to see the effects of the war on the story, but we have heard that our efforts may have changed the course of history!

November News

Faction Wars Rewards

The much-anticipated rewards for Faction Wars became available on 11/5, and came in three tiers described here by the team. We were able to claim these rewards for two weeks and the process went smoothly. Now we wait to see what comes next!


Shards

"Shards can be viewed as a participatory token that is awarded to those who participated but did not win during a Faction Wars event. At the time of deployment, Shards are not tradable for the foreseeable future, but the team has built in the functionality to allow this later on when needed!"


Coins

"Coins are soulbound tokens that cannot be traded or sold at any point in time. They can be acquired TWO different ways:

- A Claim: this is reserved for those fortunate enough to have lead their faction to victory during a season of Faction Wars

- A Burn: if you have participated in a handful of Faction Wars and have not been fortunate enough to win, you will be able to burn your participatory Shards in exchange for that season's Coin!

As coins are soulbound tokens, please ensure that you are participating in each Faction War with the desired wallet you wish to claim your coin on. At the time of deployment, 4 shards are required to burn for a coin, but this number can be altered as needed for future purposes."


Traits

"The next token within the smart contract suite are Trait tokens. Trait tokens can be acquired using either Shards or Coins. Some traits will require a different number of Shard tokens to be burnt whilst Coins in this scenario hold all the power. Holders of Coins will be able to claim any trait for free, so ensure to hustle as hard as you can towards your first Faction Wars victory!

Amongst this, traits will be used to augment your characters on our website to allow users to mix and match the aesthetic of your Isekais as you see fit. Important note: traits will not affect rarity or metadata!

Full traits functionality is still in the works, our dev team will provide updates as soon as they are available."

This was an exciting release from the team as the second official contract (or three contracts) for holders. While we can't spend these new currencies yet, we look forward to the future!

Discord Chat Bot Upgrades

The Discord chat bot was updated this month to include 3 new commands for additional fun and interaction:

  • !token (TOKENID)

  • Shows the NFT and the last sale, along with the link on OpenSea and X2Y2

  • !rank (TOKENID)

  • Shows the NFT traits and the links on OpenSea and X2Y2

  • !random

  • Shows a random Isekai NFT

Users have embraced these commands this month and have enjoyed learning more about the the many tokens in the collection!
